[MIRACULIN AND GYMEMIC ACID] — Use miraculin to bind sweet receptors and make acidic substances taste sweet, or gymemic acid to erase sweetness and make sugar taste intensely acidic.

[RISOTTO-STYLE PASTA] — Sauté dry pasta in an oil/water mixture and slowly add liquid to absorb starches, creating a creamy texture without losing starch to boiling water.

[CVAP OVEN] — Utilize this specialized oven to maintain 100% humidity during low-temperature roasting (60°C–65°C) to prevent evaporative cooling.

[MINERAL WATER FORMULATION] — Use magnesium chloride for milky sweetness, magnesium sulfate for a refreshing profile, and phosphoric acid to solubilize calcium carbonate and prevent a chalky texture.

[HYPERCARBONATION] — Use nitrous CO2 to achieve a superior carbonation level in water compared to standard methods.

[THERMO SCIENTIFIC 3L BENCHTOP CENTRIFUGE] — Clarify fruit juices after enzymatic blending or produce nut oils by spinning at ~4000 Gs.

[CENTRIFUGE STERILIZATION] — Pressure cook centrifuge buckets for 20 minutes while fully submerged to sanitize equipment sourced from non-food environments.

[TRANSGLUTAMINASE] — Sprinkle this enzyme on meat surfaces to bind cuts together in roughly four hours, storing it in a freezer to prevent moisture-induced degradation.

[ICE CHILLING] — Achieve chilling primarily through dilution, noting that larger cubes are less efficient due to a low surface area-to-volume ratio.
