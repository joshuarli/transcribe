[UHP (Ultra-high pressure pasteurization)] — Mitigates *Vibrio* risk in Gulf oysters by killing bacteria through pressure rather than heat.

[Carrageenan or Xanthan gum] — Stabilize pre-made soufflé "pucks" by setting the base and then melting at ~102°C (215°F) to allow for steam-driven expansion during baking.

[Clam Purging via Salinity-Matched Soup] — Feed clams a soup with the same salinity as the ocean to allow them to absorb specific flavors during the purging process.

[Snail Fattening (Milk or Rosemary)] — Use milk (Roman technique) or rosemary (Spanish technique) to flavor and fatten snails.

[Ascorbic Acid Powder or Sodium Metabisulfite] — Prevent avocado oxidation more effectively and with less acidity than citrus juice.

[SPL (Peelzyme)] — Enzymatically melt the inner skin and veins of peppers to remove capsaicin, though this carries the risk of dissolving the mesocarp.

[Aji Dulce] — Use this specific pepper variety to achieve a habanero-like aroma and flavor profile without the capsaicin heat.

[Horse Fat] — Use as a frying medium to produce French fries with a superior, friable exterior and a mashed-potato-like interior.

[Blender Mechanics (Tip Speed, Blade Pitch, and Cavitation)] — Select equipment based on high tip speed and specific blade pitch to maximize force and ensure proper product movement to prevent air pockets (cavitation).

[Transglutaminase + Gelatin] — Use gelatin as a "helper protein" to facilitate the cross-linking and structural bonding of vegetable tissues.
