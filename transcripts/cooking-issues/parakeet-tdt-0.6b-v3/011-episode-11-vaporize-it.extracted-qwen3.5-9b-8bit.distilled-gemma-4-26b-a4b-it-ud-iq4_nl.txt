[Vacuum Extraction (Rotovap/ISI)] — [Used to rapidly extract pine and bark flavors into water, alcohol, or oil.]

[PolyScience Smoking Gun and Herbal Vaporizers] — [The Smoking Gun is used to inject smoke into vacuum chambers for a settling aroma, whereas herbal vaporizers like the Volcano provide a cleaner, non-combustive infusion by heating material below the combustion point.]

[Maltodextrin, EnsoRite, Methylcellulose, and Xanthan Gum] — [These are used to engineer low-sugar textures: maltodextrin provides caloric bulk (with shorter chains increasing sweetness), EnsoRite converts oils into powders via its hydrophobic starch helix, methylcellulose provides meringue structure through heat-induced gelling, and xanthan gum provides thickening.]

[Habanero Infusions] — [Infusing at ~100 proof (50% alcohol) extracts fruit aroma without capsaicin heat, though red habanero infusions degrade into a jalapeño-like flavor after 2–6 months while green varieties remain stable.]

[SPL and Centrifugation] — [SPL (pectin-breaking enzyme) is used to break down pectin in fruit juices to facilitate rapid clarification when processed in a centrifuge.]
